Our Mission

Women in Tech is dedicated to supporting women entering the tech industry, especially in the field of coding. Through mentorship, events, and partnerships, our mission is to inspire and empower women to become leaders and innovators in STEM fields, with a strong focus on coding and programming.

Event Overview

Scheduled for March 15-17, 2024 this is a unique opportunity to connect with like-minded women who are passionate about coding and technology. Whether you're just starting out or looking to advance your career, our event offers invaluable workshops, mentorship sessions, and networking opportunities tailored just for you.
