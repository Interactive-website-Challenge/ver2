Venue Details

Venue: London Centre
Address: 1 Avenue, London EC2A 4NP, United Kingdom
Dates: March 15-17, 2024

How to Get There

The venue is easily accessible by public transport, car, and bike.

  • By Tube: Take the Central Line to "Liverpool Street Station." The venue is a 5-minute walk from the station.
  • By Car: Parking is limited, so we recommend using public transport. The nearest car park is NCP High Street.
  • By Bike: Bike racks are available at the venue entrance.

Nearby Accommodations

  • Innovators' Hotel: Luxury hotel located 2 blocks from the venue. Book here.
  • TechStay Inn: Affordable lodging, a 10-minute walk from the venue. Book here.
  • Urban Suites London: Boutique hotel in Shoreditch. Book here.

Explore the Area

While you're in London, take the time to visit these iconic attractions:

  • The Tower of London: A historic castle and UNESCO World Heritage Site.
  • London Eye: Enjoy panoramic views of the city from this iconic ferris wheel.
  • Borough Market: A must-visit for food enthusiasts.
