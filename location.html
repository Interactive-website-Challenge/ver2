How to Get There

The venue is easily accessible by public transport, car, and bike.

  • By Tube: Take the Central Line to "Liverpool Street Station."
    The venue is a 5-minute walk from the station.
  • By Car: Parking is limited, so we recommend using public transport.
    The nearest car park is NCP High Street.
  • By Bike: Bike racks are available at the venue entrance.

Explore the Area

While you're in London, take the time to visit these iconic attractions:

  • The Tower of London: A historic castle and UNESCO World Heritage Site.
  • London Eye: Enjoy panoramic views of the city from this iconic ferris wheel.
  • Borough Market: A must-visit for food enthusiasts.
